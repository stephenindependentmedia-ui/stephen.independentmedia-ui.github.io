Professional & Regulatory Bodies

Fully compliant. Fully committed.

Our registrations support lawful, professional and accountable delivery.

NHBRC

Supports home-building standards and consumer protection. RAFIF is registered and committed to applicable home-building requirements.

CIDB

Grades and regulates contractors. RAFIF holds a 2GB grading and works within the relevant standards and project limits.
